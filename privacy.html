Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

1. Who we are

Unblockify ("we", "us", "our") is a platform for managing student blockages in educational settings. Our contact email is hello@unblockify.app.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and organization name. This is required to create and manage your account.

Usage data

We store the content you submit — blockages, comments, cohort briefs — and associated metadata (timestamps, status, assigned instructors). This data is isolated to your organization and is never shared across organizations.

Session data

We use a single HTTP-only cookie (unblockify_token) to maintain your login session. This cookie is not readable by JavaScript and is not used for tracking.

AI interactions

When the AI Teaching Assistant is enabled, the text of blockages and relevant cohort briefs are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. No personally identifying information beyond what you include in blockage text is sent. Anthropic's Privacy Policy governs that processing.

Notifications

If you configure a Slack integration, blockage titles are sent to your Slack workspace. This is entirely optional and you control the webhook URL.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To display your information to other members of your organization (role-appropriate)
  • To generate AI responses grounded in your cohort's context
  • To send in-platform notifications about events relevant to you
  • To detect and prevent abuse

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not serve advertising.

4. Who can see your data

Data is strictly isolated per organization. Members of your organization see data appropriate to their role:

  • Students see their own blockages and cohort-level aggregate stats
  • Instructors see blockages for their assigned cohorts
  • Owners see all data within their organization

Unblockify staff may access data only to resolve support issues, with your consent, or as required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or organization, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days. You can export your data at any time via the settings page.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability (export your data in a machine-readable format)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@unblockify.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Student data (FERPA / GDPR)

When educational institutions use Unblockify, student data may be subject to FERPA, GDPR, or other applicable laws. The institution is the data controller; Unblockify acts as a data processor under your instructions. We do not use student data for any purpose outside of operating the Service for your institution.

8. Cookies

We use exactly one cookie: unblockify_token, an HTTP-only session cookie required for login. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures: HTTP-only cookies, CSRF protection, per-tenant data isolation enforced at the database level, rate limiting on authentication endpoints, and conservative security headers (X-Content-Type-Options, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy). For a full technical overview, see our Security page.

10. Changes

We will notify you of material changes via the platform or email at least 14 days in advance. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: hello@unblockify.app
