The loop

From blocked to moving in four steps.

Every blockage follows the same path. No DMs lost in a channel, no "did anyone see my message?" — just a clear signal that gets cleared.

STEP 01

Report it

Log the blockage with the context that matters — the brief, the cohort, what you've tried. Thirty seconds, done.

STEP 02

AI responds instantly

The AI Teaching Assistant replies with a Socratic nudge — grounded in your cohort's briefs and past resolutions. No waiting for a human.

STEP 03

Instructor picks it up

If the AI doesn't fully unblock you, your instructor sees it in their live queue. Nothing falls through.

STEP 04

Signal goes green

They pick the right kind of help and leave a note. Your signal flips green, the moment gets recorded, and you're moving again.
