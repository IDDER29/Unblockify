FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you pick a plan.

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. Starter is free forever for a single cohort — full report-to-resolve lifecycle and the AI assistant included. No card required, no trial clock. It's the real product, not a teaser.

What counts as an active student?

A student who reports or comments on at least one blockage in a billing month. Enrolled-but-quiet students never get billed — you pay for momentum, not headcount.

Do we need our own AI key?

No. The AI Teaching Assistant runs out of the box with a built-in local fallback. Bring your own ANTHROPIC_API_KEY to switch on the full Claude-powered experience whenever you like.

Can we self-host and keep data local?

Absolutely — Unblockify is local-first. It runs as a single Node server on a local SQLite database, so your students' data can stay entirely on your own infrastructure.

How does billing work?

Team is billed monthly based on active students that month — usage scales up and down with each cohort automatically. Organization plans are invoiced annually; just talk to us for a quote.
