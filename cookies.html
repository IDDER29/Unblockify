Cookie Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Short version: Unblockify uses exactly one cookie. It's required for login. We don't use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies of any kind.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file stored in your browser by a website. Cookies are used for things like keeping you logged in, remembering preferences, and tracking behaviour across sites.

The one cookie we use

Name Purpose Type Duration
unblockify_token Keeps you logged in. Contains a JWT with your user ID, org, and role. Strictly necessary Session (cleared on logout)

This cookie is:

  • HTTP-only — JavaScript cannot read it, which prevents XSS token theft
  • Secure — only sent over HTTPS in production
  • SameSite=Lax — provides CSRF protection
  • Not a tracking cookie — it contains no advertising identifiers or cross-site tracking data

What we don't use

  • No Google Analytics or other analytics cookies
  • No advertising or retargeting cookies
  • No social media tracking pixels
  • No third-party cookies of any kind

Do you need to accept cookies?

The single cookie we use is strictly necessary to log in to the application. Without it, authentication is impossible. Because it is strictly necessary, we don't require a consent banner for it under most privacy regulations (GDPR Article 5(3) exception for technically necessary cookies).

If you use Unblockify as a guest on the public marketing pages (this page, the homepage, pricing, etc.), no cookies are set at all.

Managing cookies

You can clear the unblockify_token cookie at any time via your browser's developer tools or privacy settings — this will log you out. You can also click "Log out" in the application, which deletes the cookie server-side.

Changes

If we ever introduce additional cookies, we'll update this page and provide notice in the application at least 14 days in advance.
