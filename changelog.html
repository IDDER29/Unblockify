What's new

Changelog

We ship often. Here's every release, in reverse chronological order — features, fixes, and the thinking behind them.

24 Jun 2026
v1.8.0

Check-ins, anonymous reporting, and PWA support

  • NewInstructors and owners can flag students for check-in with an optional note. Check-in history appears on every student profile.
  • NewStudents can report blockages anonymously — name is masked for all staff in list and detail views.
  • NewInternal comments: staff can leave private notes on blockages that students never see.
  • NewSlack integration: owners can configure a webhook URL so every new blockage posts to a Slack channel.
  • NewStudent portfolio export: download your full blockage history as a JSON file from the Growth page.
  • NewPWA support: Unblockify can now be installed as an app on mobile and desktop (manifest + service worker).
  • NewPlan & Billing page with Free/Pro/Enterprise plan comparison.
  • ImprovedOnboarding checklist on the owner dashboard — dismissable once all setup steps are done.
  • ImprovedBrief versioning: cohort briefs now maintain a full edit history with timestamps.
10 Jun 2026
v1.7.0

Multi-turn AI follow-ups and instructor copilot

  • NewStudents can ask the AI Teaching Assistant up to 2 follow-up questions on any blockage, keeping the Socratic dialogue alive without instructor involvement.
  • NewInstructor copilot: "Draft a reply" button generates an AI-suggested response that instructors can edit before sending.
  • NewStudent growth fingerprint on the Growth page: shows blockage patterns, resolution time trend, and top topics over time.
  • NewKnowledge library: students can browse resolved blockages in their cohort as searchable Q&A.
  • ImprovedAt-risk radar now includes students without a cohort assignment.
22 May 2026
v1.6.0

Analytics, CSAT, and blockage export

  • NewStudent satisfaction scores (CSAT): students rate resolved blockages 1–5 stars. Averages visible per instructor and org-wide.
  • NewCSV export: owners can download all blockages for a date range.
  • NewAI deflection rate analytics: see what percentage of blockages the AI resolved without human intervention and estimated instructor hours saved.
  • NewActivity feed: recent status events across the whole org, for owners.
  • ImprovedBy-cohort analytics now shows open, in-support, and resolved counts per cohort side by side.
  • FixBlockage list pagination now preserves filters when navigating between pages.
5 May 2026
v1.5.0

Cohort briefs and AI grounding

  • NewCohort briefs: owners and instructors can write a brief that grounds the AI's responses in the specific curriculum, stack, and week of the cohort.
  • NewThe AI Teaching Assistant now retrieves the most relevant past resolved blockages and uses them to inform its Socratic response.
  • NewInstructor assignment to cohorts: instructors only see the queue for cohorts they're assigned to.
  • ImprovedBlockage report form now shows "X students in your cohort have been stuck this term" — social proof that normalizes asking for help.
15 Apr 2026
v1.0.0

Initial launch

  • NewMulti-tenant sign-up: any organization creates an isolated workspace in under a minute.
  • NewBlockage lifecycle: report → claim → resolve → reopen, with a full comment thread and status timeline.
  • NewAI Teaching Assistant: immediate Socratic response to every new blockage.
  • NewThree roles: owner, instructor, student — each with a role-appropriate view.
  • NewInvite system: generate invite links/codes; students and instructors join via join.html?code=….
  • NewNotification system: in-app notifications for all role-relevant events.
