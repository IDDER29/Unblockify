Why Unblockify exists

A blockage that lingers is the first step out the door.

In bootcamps, coding schools, and training programs, students get blocked constantly. Today that help lives in noisy chat channels and DMs — requests get buried, no one owns them, nothing is measured, and the same blocker gets re-solved ten times over.

Here's the insight we built the company around: a blockage that lingers becomes frustration. Frustration becomes disengagement. And disengagement, left alone, becomes attrition — a student who never comes back. It almost never announces itself. It just goes quiet.

Unblockify makes the path visible and short. Every "I'm stuck" becomes a tracked object that moves from Blocked → In support → Resolved — answered in minutes, owned by exactly one queue, and measured by default. We turn the quietest, most expensive moment in learning into resolved, measured momentum.

The bet we're making

AI handles the repetitive 80%. Your humans get the 20% that needs them.

We're building for a world where an AI can resolve the question that's been asked forty times before — instantly, day or night, without judgment. That's not a threat to great instructors. It's the best thing that's ever happened to them.

When the trivial blockers are deflected before they ever reach a human, an instructor's expertise flows to the work that actually demands it: the student who's genuinely stuck, the conceptual gap, the one who's about to give up. Our AI Teaching Assistant is that wedge — a Socratic first responder grounded in your cohort and brief, building a knowledge base from every resolution it sees. AI-first, human-where-it-counts. That's the bet.

What we believe

Four convictions, baked into every screen.

Momentum over tickets

A ticket number in a void doesn't help anyone. A student needs to keep moving. We optimize for the next step taken, not the request logged — and time-to-unblock is our north-star metric.

Status is the brand

Every object answers one question: blocked, in support, or flowing? Our "Signal" design language — the pulse line, signal green, coral — makes state legible at a glance, for everyone, all the time.

Retention is a product, not a report

Keeping students isn't a chart you read after they've left. It's an outcome you design for — by catching the at-risk signal early and closing the gap before it widens into a goodbye.

Local-first and trustworthy

Unblockify runs entirely on your own machine, with its own database. Your students' data stays with you. External services are optional upgrades, never dependencies — the app always runs and always demos.

Built for any organization

One platform. Every kind of place that teaches.

Unblockify is multi-tenant from the ground up. Any organization self-registers, gets a fully isolated workspace, and brings its own people, cohorts, and brand. Every row is scoped to your org and the boundary is enforced server-side — your data is never visible across it.
