The wedge

An AI Teaching Assistant on every blockage.

The instant a student reports a blockage, the assistant replies — never with the answer, always with the next question. It's grounded in the cohort, the brief, and a knowledge base that builds itself from every problem your team has already solved.

Instant Socratic reply

Every new blockage gets a thoughtful first response in seconds — guiding questions, not spoilers. Students stay unblocked even when no instructor is online.

Grounded in your cohort

Answers are conditioned on the cohort and its brief, so the help matches what students are actually building — not generic advice scraped from the open web.

A knowledge base that builds itself

Every resolved blockage feeds retrieval. The next student with a similar problem gets an answer informed by how your team solved it last time — accuracy compounds as you go.

"This unblocked me" deflection

When the assistant gets a student moving again, one tap marks the blockage AI-resolved. That deflection is measured, reported, and turned into hours your instructors get back.

Instructor copilot

When a human is needed, the assistant drafts a reply in your voice — grounded in the thread and past resolutions. Instructors edit and send in seconds instead of starting from a blank box.

Auto-triage on arrival

Each blockage is classified the moment it lands — difficulty, topics, and urgency — so the queue sorts itself and the right people see the right problems first.

Multi-turn follow-ups

The conversation doesn't stop at one reply. The assistant follows the thread, responds to what the student tries next, and keeps nudging until they're flowing.

Thread summaries

Long back-and-forth? The assistant distills it into a tight summary, so an instructor picking up the blockage is caught up in a glance.

Weekly struggle digest

Owners get a "what your cohort struggled with this week" report — the recurring blockers, the topics on fire, and where curriculum needs attention.

The blockage lifecycle

From "I'm stuck" to resolved — on rails.

Every blockage moves through one clear path. No DMs lost in a channel, no "did anyone see my message?" — just a status everyone can read at a glance.

STEP 01

Report & auto-reply

A student logs the blockage with context. The AI Teaching Assistant replies instantly and the blockage is auto-triaged into the queue.

STEP 02

Claim

An instructor for that cohort claims it. The signal flips to "in support" — the student knows a real person is on it, in real time.

STEP 03

Resolve & reopen

Resolve with a note that's kept forever. If it wasn't fully fixed, reopen in one tap — nothing gets quietly dropped.

Threaded conversations

Full markdown, fenced code blocks, and screenshot attachments — the whole exchange lives in one place, readable and searchable.

Real-time live updates

Status changes, new replies, and claims push to every open board instantly. The room always reflects reality without a refresh.

A timeline you can trust

Created, claimed, commented, resolved — every event is logged. The history of how a blockage was cleared is never lost.

Analytics & retention

See the room. Catch the slip before it happens.

Unblockify turns support into signal. Owners see exactly where the cohort is stuck, what the AI is handling, and which students need a human before they disengage.

The headline number: what share of blockages the assistant cleared on its own, and the instructor-hours that bought back — measured, not estimated.

At-risk students radar

CSAT on every resolution

Students rate how their blockage was handled. Satisfaction trends by cohort and instructor tell you what "good help" actually looks like.

Student-360 profiles

One page per student: their blockages, their patterns, their momentum over time. Context-rich check-ins instead of guesswork.

By cohort & instructor

Resolve rate, median time-to-unblock, and volume broken down by cohort and by instructor — find the bottleneck before it costs a sprint.

Median time-to-unblock

The metric that defines momentum. Watch it drop as the assistant and your team get faster — and as the knowledge base deepens.

Multi-tenant workspaces
